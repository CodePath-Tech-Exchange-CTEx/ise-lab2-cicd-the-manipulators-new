Usability testing for Challenges 
User personas: 
Persona 1: Jimmy, 22, An occasional runner who wants to get consistent with running goals while being busy with college
o Background: Jimmy is a full-time college student who wants to train for a marathon.
o Goals: Understand how much he has run, where he needs to be in terms of distance and how will he get there 
o Frustrations: Not knowing if he is on track with distance and properly training on a consistent pattern / routine 
Test tasks: 
o Task: Navigate the home page (Look for buttons like tabs) (5 secs) 
o Task: Navigate to the analytics tab and remember how to get back to the main page and where to see posts (5 secs) 
Success criteria: 
• Did the participants complete the tasks successfully within the time frame? 
• How long did each participant spend ? 
• Is their average satisfaction rating above 4 out of 5 ?
• Is navigation easy? 4 out of 5 accessibility rating?
Test environment: 
• The test will be conducted virtually over discord 
• The moderator will share their screen and the user will evaluate the page and tell the moderator their thoughts out loud

He also realizes that the goal ETA should be specified and remind the user on what it is as opposed to just saying “Goal ETA” and inserting the time.
